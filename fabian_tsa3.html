ANNOUNCEMENT

NO FACE TO FACE CLASSES ON MAY 18 AND 20


Due to heavy rain, FEU Tech will conduct synchronous online classes on May 18 and 20, 2024. Students are advised to check their professor's announcements for further instructions and to keep up with the classworks via Canvas.


This measure is taken to ensure the safety of all students. Stay safe during this period of harsh weather. For any more updates, please check the official FEU Tech Facebook page.


Offices will be open on-site to assist you with other transactions. You may visit the respective offices. Our staff will be available to help you with enrollment, documents, and other inquiries. Please follow the safety protocols of the campus. We appreciate your cooperation and understanding during this hard time. Please feel free to reach out for any assistance you might need.


Stay safe Tamaraws!
